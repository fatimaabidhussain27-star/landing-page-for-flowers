Our Story

Founded in 2010, Blossom & Petal began as a small neighborhood flower shop with a passion for bringing beauty into people's lives. Our founder, Isabella Rose, believed that flowers have the power to transform spaces and uplift spirits.

Today, we continue that tradition by sourcing the freshest, most beautiful flowers from local growers and around the world. Each arrangement is carefully crafted by our skilled florists to tell a unique story and create lasting memories.

We're committed to sustainable practices and supporting our community, ensuring that every purchase from Blossom & Petal makes a positive impact.
